# Pre-call intake — AI Identity discovery

Hi {{ name }},

Looking forward to {{ day }}. To make our 45-60 minutes useful, three quick
asks. Send back what's easy to send; we'll cover the rest live.

## 1. Agent inventory (most useful)

A list of AI agents your team is currently running or seriously planning
within the next 6 months. For each, even one line each is enough:

- **Name / purpose** — what does the agent do?
- **Owner** — which team or person runs it?
- **What it touches** — APIs, databases, customer data, money?
- **Authority level** — read-only, can write, can act autonomously, or human-in-the-loop?
- **Status** — prototype, internal pilot, customer-facing, production?

CSV, spreadsheet link, or a paragraph in the reply — all fine.

## 2. Three risk scenarios

Top three "what could go wrong" scenarios that keep you up at night about
agents in your environment. Doesn't have to be hypothetical — recent
near-misses count and are the most useful data.

## 3. Existing stack

A quick read on what's already in place:

- Identity / IAM provider (Okta, Entra, Auth0, etc.)
- SIEM / log aggregation (Splunk, Sentinel, Datadog, etc.)
- Compliance frameworks you're tracking (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, etc.)
- Cloud provider primarily (AWS / GCP / Azure / on-prem)

---

That's it. If any of this is too much to assemble before the call, just send
what's easy. The point is to walk in informed, not to gatekeep your time.
